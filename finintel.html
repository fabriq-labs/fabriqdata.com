The Challenge

Three domains where finance teams lose the most time

Legacy systems and manual processes create cascading errors, delayed closes, and lost revenue across every industry.

Retailer Deductions & Short-pay Reconciliation

Consumer goods brands experience significant annual losses from unvalidated deductions by retail chains. Matching deductions to delivery proof and disputing chargebacks remains entirely manual — burning hours and missing deadlines.

Insurance Commission Reconciliation

Carrier statement formats vary wildly — no two are the same. Insurance agencies spend days every month manually matching statement rows to policy records. Errors compound and disputes are difficult to trace.

Cross-Border Consolidation

Companies with foreign subsidiaries close books days or weeks late. Intercompany eliminations happen in Excel, multi-currency revaluations are error-prone, and GAAP reconciliation requires manual intervention every period.

What We Do

AI That Extracts, Classifies, Matches & Reconciles at Scale

FinIntel deploys purpose-built AI agents trained on the specific document types and workflows that break finance teams — not generic LLMs bolted onto spreadsheets.

  • Classify and match line items across systems with high precision
  • Flag exceptions and generate audit-ready dispute packages automatically
  • Close books faster with automated intercompany eliminations and GAAP reconciliation
  • Finance-grade compliance and full auditability at every step
